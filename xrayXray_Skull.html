Cranial Region (8 bones)

Frontal Bone
Frontal Diagram

Location: Forehead region

Function: Protects frontal lobes, forms upper orbits

Parietal Bones (2)
Parietal Diagram

Location: Sides and roof of cranium

Function: Forms cranial vault, protects parietal lobes

Temporal Bones (2)
Temporal Diagram

Location: Sides and base of skull

Function: Houses ear structures, forms TMJ

Occipital Bone
Occipital Diagram

Location: Posterior skull base

Function: Forms posterior fossa, contains foramen magnum

Sphenoid Bone
Sphenoid Diagram

Location: Base of skull

Function: Connects cranial and facial bones

Ethmoid Bone
Ethmoid Diagram

Location: Between orbits

Function: Forms nasal cavity, medial orbit wall

Facial Region (14 bones)

Maxilla Bones (2)
Maxilla Diagram

Location: Upper jaw

Function: Forms upper jaw, contains upper teeth

Zygomatic Bones (2)
Zygomatic Diagram

Location: Cheekbones

Function: Forms cheek prominences, part of orbit

Nasal Bones (2)
Nasal Diagram

Location: Bridge of nose

Function: Forms nasal bridge, protects nasal cavity

Lacrimal Bones (2)
Lacrimal Diagram

Location: Medial orbit wall

Function: Contains lacrimal fossa for tear drainage

Palatine Bones (2)
Palatine Diagram

Location: Posterior hard palate

Function: Forms posterior hard palate

Inferior Nasal Conchae (2)
Conchae Diagram

Location: Nasal cavity walls

Function: Increases nasal surface area

Vomer Bone
Vomer Diagram

Location: Nasal septum

Function: Forms inferior nasal septum

Mandible Bone
Mandible Diagram

Location: Lower jaw

Function: Only movable skull bone, contains lower teeth
